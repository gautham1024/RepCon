Building and Restoring Confidence


Repcon (Repairs to Concrete) was established in the year 1992. The aim of the organization is to do justice to the most neglected field in the civil industry i.e. concrete Repairs and restoration of structures. We at Repcon use the best of Techniques, materials, and methods available to restore structures with the belief that ‘A repair when done should be done the first time in the best way’. We with our single-minded dedication have achieved Concrete reputation in concrete restoration. In the year 1994, Repcon has diversified into concrete cutting and we now proud owners of the latest in Diamond Cutting Technology including core cutting. Floor sawing, wall sawing, and wire sawing machine. We are in the process of continuously upgrading our knowledge, equipment, and techniques and specialize in.
